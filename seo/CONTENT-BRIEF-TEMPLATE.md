## 5. Voice check (10x Velocity editorial rules)

- Declarative. Never "we think," "might," "could potentially."
- Specific over fluffy. Every claim has a number or a name attached.
- Banned: "digital transformation," "unlock your potential," "next-gen," "AI-powered everything," "book a free consultation," "get started now," "in today's fast-paced world."
- Honest comparisons allowed and encouraged — including when a competitor or a cheaper option is the right call.
